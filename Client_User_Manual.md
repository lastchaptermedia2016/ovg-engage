## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Accept Your Invitation
You will receive an email invitation from Omniverge Global.
1.  Click the **Accept Invitation** button in the email
2.  Create your secure password
3.  Login to your Client Console at `https://ovg-engage.vercel.app/login`

> **Important:** If you don't see the invitation email, please check your spam folder. If still not found, contact our support team.

## 🎨 Customizing Your Widget

Make the chat widget feel like a seamless part of your website:

### Upload Your Logo
1.  Go to **Widget Settings → Branding**
2.  Enter your logo URL or upload directly
3.  Save changes

### Header & Footer Graphics
You can fully customize the widget appearance:
- ✅ Upload custom header background image
- ✅ Upload custom footer background image
- ✅ Adjust background overlay intensity for text readability
- ✅ Set your brand primary color and accents

All changes update live in the preview window.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### I didn't receive my invitation email
1.  Check your spam / junk mail folder
2.  Add `@omnivergeglobal.com` to your safe senders list
3.  Contact OmniVerge Global Support for assistance

### I can't login
- Ensure you're using the email address where you received the invitation
- Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page
